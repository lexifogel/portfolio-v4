English Alphabet Poster

New Concepts: Composition, Grid-based Layout, Trims and Bleeds

Adobe Photoshop, Illustrator


For this assignment, we were challenged to create an alphabet poster with a unique theme. As an avid soup lover, this idea came to me naturally. Although it was difficult to compile images for each letter taken from similar angles and with a consistent color scheme, I aimed to maintain unity through the cropping and typography of each cell.

Layered Collage Poster

New Concepts: Masking, Layering, Typography

Adobe Photoshop


In this assignment, we were challenged to use a combination of flowers and text to produce a collage layout. These designs merge botanical illustrations with bold typography by layering the leaves, flower stems, petals around the letters, creating by this a sense of depth and movement in the composition.
